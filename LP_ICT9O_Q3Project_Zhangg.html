what to do during a fire?

A fire can happen anywhere and it’s important to always be prepared for what you would do if you had one in your home. Likewise, it’s just as important to look at all of the ways that you can prevent one from happening in the first place. You want to know how to prevent fire in your home to protect it and the ones you love. Of course, there are the more obvious things that you can buy to protect your home against fire, such as smoke alarms, fire extinguishers, having an escape plan, and even fireproof safe deposit boxes to protect your valuables.

dos don'ts
know your building’s evacuation plan. don't use elevators during an evacuation.
evacuate calmly and quickly whenever a fire alarm or carbon monoxide alarm sounds. don't ignore any building alarms. If you hear a fire alarm or carbon monoxide alarm, look into it.
if you encounter smoke during your evacuation, stay low to the floor. don't leave candles, incense, barbecue grills or other open flames unattended.
before opening a door, feel it with the back of your hand. If the door is hot, do not open it. don't leave candles, incense, barbecue grills or other open flames unattended.
